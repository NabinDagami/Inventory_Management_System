Title: Build a Modern Windows Inventory, Sales & Purchase Management App
Tech Stack: Python 3.x, CustomTkinter (CTk), SQLite3, Pillow (for images/icons), Matplotlib (for charts), ReportLab (for PDF)

Core Features

Inventory Management

Products with Category & Brand

Auto-generated SKU (e.g., CAT-BRAND-001)

Dual Pricing: Normal & Workshop

Stock tracking with reorder alerts

Sales Module

Create sales (Cash/Credit)

Dynamic pricing based on customer type

Add multiple products to cart

Generate Invoice (PDF/Print)

Update stock automatically

Purchases Module

Record purchases (Cash/Credit)

Multiple product entries with cost price

Auto-update stock

Supplier credit balance tracking

Customers & Suppliers

Customers: Normal & Workshop

Credit balance & contact info

Suppliers: Name, contact, address, purchase history

Reports

Sales report (date range)

Purchases report

Stock report (low stock)

Profit & loss report

Customer & supplier ledger (credit history)

User Authentication (Optional)

Admin & Staff roles

Login screen with CTk styling

Professional UI/UX (CustomTkinter)

Sidebar Navigation (collapsible)

Dashboard

Settings

Top Bar:

App title / Logo

User profile

Light/Dark theme toggle (built-in CTk feature)

Dashboard Widgets:

Total stock, Today’s sales, Purchases, Profit

Low stock alerts

Chart: Sales vs Purchases (Matplotlib embedded in CTkCanvas)

Forms & Tables:

CTkEntry, CTkOptionMenu, CTkTable (or custom table using CTkFrame + labels)

Modal dialogs for add/edit (CTkToplevel)

Search & filter in tables

Database Schema (SQLite)

(Same as we finalized earlier — supplier_id removed from products)

categories: category_id, category_name

brands: brand_id, brand_name

products: product_id, name, category_id, brand_id, sku, stock, price_normal, price_workshop, reorder_level

customers: customer_id, name, type (Normal/Workshop), contact, credit_balance

suppliers: supplier_id, name, contact, address

purchases & purchase_items

sales & sale_items

users (optional)

Business Rules

SKU auto-generated based on category & brand

Stock increases on purchase, decreases on sale

Customer type decides pricing

Reorder alerts displayed on dashboard

Credit sales/purchases update customer/supplier balance

Output Requirements

Full Python project using CustomTkinter (CTk)

Modular code: /modules/products.py, /modules/sales.py, etc.

SQLite database with migrations and sample data

PDF invoice generation (ReportLab)

Dark/Light theme support
